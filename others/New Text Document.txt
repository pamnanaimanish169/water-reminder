Features:

- Input water intake(in ml or oz or a glass)
- How much water should you drink for each day
- Reminder to drink water in intervals of time(real time notifications)
- Real-time notifications when the user hasn't opened a new tab for some time
and therefore not tracked their water intake levels for a specified period.

Note: Feature based on priority i.e. step wise
